Frequently asked

How do I connect with my partner?

From the pairing screen, share your invite code with your partner, or enter the code they send you. Once one of you enters the other's code, your two accounts connect into a single shared planet.

Can I add photos from my camera, or only my library?

Both. When you add a memory, LiyoPlanet lets you take a new photo or choose one (or several) from your existing library.

What happens if we disconnect our planets?

Disconnecting is permanent. Your planets are separated, and the shared memories, photos, and comments tied to that pairing are removed for both accounts. There's no way to undo this, so we'd suggest being sure before you do it.

I forgot my password — what do I do?

On the login screen, tap “Forgot Password?” and enter your email. We'll send you a link to reset it.

Is my data private?

Your memories are visible only to you and your paired partner — never to other LiyoPlanet users. See our Privacy Policy for the full details on what we collect and how it's used.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at support@lsquared.sa from your account's email address and we'll take care of it.
